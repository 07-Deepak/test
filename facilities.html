Society's Working Committee Members

The members selected for the working committee have been chosen based on their consistent dedication, time, and sincere efforts in engaging with the entire society. They have personally approached residents, listened to individual concerns, and compiled a comprehensive list of issues affecting the community. Their active participation in the society handover meetings organized by the builder reflects their commitment to representing the interests of all members. These individuals have contributed meaningfully by leveraging their professional experience, knowledge, and educational background to ensure a smooth transition and informed decision-making. Their proactive approach and willingness to take responsibility make them valuable representatives in guiding the society toward better governance and collective progress.

It is important to note that these members are not receiving any salary, commission, or financial benefit for the roles assigned to them. They have volunteered to serve free of cost, fully aware that it is a selfless and often thankless job. Despite this, they remain determined to work diligently for the betterment of all residents and the overall welfare of the society.
